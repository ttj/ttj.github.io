About

Taylor T. Johnson

Associate Professor of Computer Science

Associate Dean for Graduate Education

College of Connected Computing (CCC)

Vanderbilt University

Taylor T. Johnson is Associate Professor of Computer Science (CS) and Associate Dean for Graduate Education in the College of Connected Computing (CCC) at Vanderbilt University.

His research interests include cyber-physical systems, formal methods, verification and validation, autonomy, and safety-critical systems.

Research

My research focuses on developing formal methods and verification techniques for cyber-physical systems and autonomous systems.
